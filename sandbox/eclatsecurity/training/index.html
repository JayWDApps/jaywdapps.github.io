Comprehensive Training & Preparation

Prepare yourself for a robust and all-encompassing security training experience with our Comprehensive Security Training and Preparation program. In an ever-evolving landscape of threats and vulnerabilities, staying ahead requires a deep understanding of security practices and the ability to adapt to new challenges.

Our program is meticulously designed to provide you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to safeguard assets, mitigate risks, and respond effectively to security incidents. Whether you are a novice looking to establish a strong foundation or an experienced professional aiming to refine your expertise, this training is tailored to meet your specific needs.

Active Shooter Training
In today's world, being prepared for unexpected threats is of paramount importance. E’CLAT’s Active Shooter Training program is designed to equip individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills needed to respond effectively in the event of an active shooter incident.

Who Should Attend?

This training is suitable for individuals, businesses, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and any organization or group concerned about safety and security. Preparedness is a shared responsibility, and our program can help you take proactive steps towards a safer future.

What Will You Learn?

  1. Life-Saving Skills: Gain essential life-saving skills to protect yourself and others during an active shooter situation. Learn how to make informed decisions under pressure.
  2. Emergency Procedures: Understand the importance of quick and decisive action. Learn how to recognize the signs of an active shooter, how to alert authorities, and how to communicate with others during a crisis.
  3. De-escalation Techniques: Explore strategies for de-escalating potentially violent situations before they escalate into an active shooter scenario.
  4. Customized Solutions: Our training can be tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization, ensuring that your staff is well-prepared to respond to threats in your unique environment.
Workplace Violence Training

Who Should Attend?

This training is suitable for individuals, businesses, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and any organization or group concerned about safety and security. Preparedness is a shared responsibility, and our program can help you take proactive steps towards a safer future.

What Will You Learn?

Illinois 28-Hour Armed Training

Illinois Concealed Carry & Concealed Carry Renewal Training
The Illinois Concealed Carry Training program is your gateway to legally carrying a concealed firearm in the state while ensuring safety, responsibility, and adherence to state regulations. Whether you're a first-time applicant or seeking to renew your concealed carry license, our comprehensive training program covers all the essential aspects of concealed carry.

Who Should Attend?

This training is essential for individuals applying for their initial Illinois concealed carry license and those seeking to renew their existing licenses. It is also valuable for anyone interested in concealed carry as a means of self-defense.

What Will You Learn?

  1. Firearms Safety: Master firearm safety fundamentals, including safe handling, storage, and maintenance of your concealed carry firearm.
  2. Use of Force: Explore the principles of the use of force continuum and when and how to use force legally and responsibly.
  3. Concealment and Carry Techniques: Learn effective concealment methods and holsters, as well as best practices for carrying concealed in various situations.
  4. Scenario-Based Training: Participate in realistic scenario-based training exercises that simulate real-world concealed carry situations.
CEW Training (Taser)
CEW Training, also known as Conducted Electrical Weapon Training, provides essential knowledge and skills to law enforcement officers, security personnel, and other professionals in the safe and effective use of conducted electrical weapons, commonly known as tasers. This specialized training program equips individuals with the expertise to handle and deploy these non-lethal devices responsibly and within the bounds of the law.

Who Should Attend?

CEW Training is crucial for law enforcement and security professionals, enabling them to respond effectively to threats while prioritizing safety for all parties involved. By combining theoretical knowledge with practical skills, CEW Training ensures that officers are well-prepared to handle diverse and challenging situations while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and safety.

What Will You Learn?

  1. Legal and Ethical Considerations: Understanding the legal and ethical guidelines surrounding the use of conducted electrical weapons, ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  2. Device Familiarization: Comprehensive training on the operation, features, and limitations of conducted electrical weapons to ensure proper usage.
  3. Risk Assessment: Evaluating situations to determine the appropriate use of a conducted electrical weapon, considering factors such as threat level, individual behavior, and officer safety.
  4. Proper Deployment: Training on accurate aiming, effective deployment techniques, and distance considerations to maximize the device's effectiveness.
Florida Concealed Carry Training
The Florida Concealed Carry Training program is your passport to legally carry a concealed firearm for self-defense and personal protection within the state. Whether you're a first-time applicant or seeking to renew your concealed carry license, our comprehensive training program ensures you're well-prepared to exercise your Second Amendment rights responsibly and safely.

Who Should Attend?

This training is essential for individuals applying for their initial Florida concealed carry license and those seeking to renew their existing licenses. It is also valuable for anyone interested in concealed carry as a means of self-defense and personal safety.

What Will You Learn?

  1. Legal Understanding: Gain a thorough understanding of Florida's concealed carry laws and regulations, including legal rights, responsibilities, and where you can and cannot carry concealed.
  2. Use of Force and Conflict Resolution: Explore the use of force continuum and responsible use of your firearm, emphasizing conflict avoidance and de-escalation techniques.
  3. Concealment Techniques: Learn effective concealment methods, holster choices, and carry positions to comfortably and discreetly carry your firearm in various situations.
  4. Scenario-Based Training: Engage in scenario-based training exercises that simulate real-world concealed carry scenarios, helping you apply your skills and decision-making under pressure.
